# Retail Sales Intelligence Dashboard
**Tools:** Power BI | DAX | Excel  
**Data:** Sample Superstore Dataset (Kaggle)

## Overview
A 3-page interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales
performance, product profitability, and shipping operations
across 4 U.S. regions.

## Key Findings
- Technology drives highest sales but Office Supplies has best margin
- Tables and Bookcases are consistent loss leaders despite high sales
- Standard Class shipping used for 60%+ of orders
- Q4 drives highest revenue — November peak across all years

## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Overview — KPIs, monthly trends, regional breakdown, YoY growth
2. Product Performance — Sales vs profit scatter, top 10 products, sub-category losses
3. Shipping & Operations — Days to ship, discount impact, ship mode breakdown

## DAX Measures
Total Sales · Total Profit · Profit Margin % · Sales YoY % ·
Sales LY · Sales MTD · Sales YTD · Loss Orders · Avg Discount
